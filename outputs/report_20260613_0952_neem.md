## 5. Challenges & Controversies
Despite the potential benefits of neem, there are several challenges and controversies surrounding its use, including:
* The potential for neem oil to be toxic to humans and animals if not used properly ([National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences](https://www.niehs.nih.gov/health/topics/agents/neem/index.cfm))
* The lack of standardization in neem products, which can lead to varying levels of quality and efficacy ([Journal of Pharmacy and Pharmacology](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1111/jphp.12444))
* The potential for neem to interact with other medications, which can lead to adverse effects ([Journal of Ethnopharmacology](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0378874113003345))
* The environmental impact of large-scale neem cultivation, including the potential for deforestation and water pollution ([Environmental Science & Technology](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/es501654q))

These challenges and controversies highlight the need for further research and regulation to ensure the safe and effective use of neem.
